Key finding: the $20 tier has converged

ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, Gemini Advanced and Perplexity Pro all sit at ~$19.99–$20/mo with near-identical feature sets (web search, file upload, image gen). You are no longer paying for *capability* differences at this tier — you're paying for *interface* and *ecosystem*. That means the real savings are in two places:

  • Drop to a free tier if your use is light (DeepSeek, Mistral Le Chat, and the free tiers of all four majors cost $0).
  • Drop to a specialist like Rytr ($9) if you only need writing, not a general assistant.

When ChatGPT is still worth $20

If you rely on GPT-4o's multimodal + custom GPTs + the largest third-party plugin ecosystem, none of the cheaper options fully replace it. For everyone else, the table above shows where the money leaks.

Verdict

  • Cheapest full alternative: DeepSeek / Mistral Le Chat (free).
  • Cheapest paid writing tool: Rytr ($9).
  • Best "better than ChatGPT" for long-form: Claude Pro ($20) — same price, stronger on documents.
